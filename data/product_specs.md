# Northwind Router NW-3000 Product Specifications

## Overview
The NW-3000 is a dual-band mesh router for small offices, running NorthwindOS firmware version 4.2.1.

## Ports and hardware
The NW-3000 has four gigabit Ethernet ports, one USB-C port, and a WPS button. Maximum throughput is 2400 Mbps on the 5 GHz band.

## Common error codes
ERR_4521 means the device cannot reach the DHCP server; restart the modem. ERR_3300 indicates a firmware mismatch; update to version 4.2.1 or later. ERR_1002 is a hardware fault requiring an RMA.

## Warranty
The NW-3000 includes a two-year limited warranty. Register the serial number at register.northwind.example within 30 days of purchase.
