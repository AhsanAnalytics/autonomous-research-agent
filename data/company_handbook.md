# Northwind Remote Work Handbook

## Working hours
Employees set their own schedule but must overlap with the team core hours of 10am to 3pm Pacific Time. We do not track hours; we track outcomes.

## Vacation and leave
Full-time employees receive 25 days of paid vacation per year, plus local public holidays. Trips under five days do not need manager approval. Unused vacation rolls over up to 10 days into the next year.

## Equipment
Every new hire receives a company laptop of their choice (MacBook Pro or a Linux ThinkPad) and a 400 dollar budget for a monitor, keyboard, and chair. Replacement laptops are provided every three years.

## Security
Never store customer data on personal devices. All company accounts must use the password manager and two-factor authentication. Report any lost device to security@northwind.example within 24 hours.

## Expenses
Reimbursable expenses include home internet up to 60 dollars per month, coworking space membership, and books related to your role. Submit receipts through the expenses portal by the end of each month.
